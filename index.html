📌 Quick Reminders

  • Always verify medication instructions with the chart before relaying to patients.
  • For urgent symptoms (spreading infection, severe allergic reaction), direct patients to call 911 or go to the ER immediately.
  • Never interpret biopsy results to a patient without physician review first.
  • Document all patient phone communications in the chart.

🔬 Biopsy Report Interpreter

Paste a pathology report below. The tool will identify the diagnosis and provide a plain-language explanation and patient-friendly talking points for your use after physician review.

⚠️ Important: Always have the physician review results before discussing with the patient. This tool is a reference aid, not a substitute for clinical judgment.

💡 Tips for Best Results

  • Paste the full report including diagnosis, margins, and special findings.
  • Include depth measurements (mm) if present — these affect melanoma staging.
  • If the report is unclear, flag it for physician clarification before calling the patient.
